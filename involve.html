Omega Psi is a National Honor Society for Undergraduates in Cognitive Science.

Omega Psi is dedicated to raising awareness of and involvement in cognitive science, within and beyond institutions sheltering chapters. The organization encourages research, networking, and community outreach among students, faculty, and professionals in cognitive science and related fields. It also recognizes undergraduates who achieve academic excellence.

CSSA is an Omega Psi chapter that offers Honors. Honors Members are recognized by Omega Psi to be academically excellent. It requires:

  • 3.0 cumulative GPA, 3.5 major GPA
  • Completed 3 semesters at UCSD and taken 3 cognitive science course
  • Commit to doing 40 hours of research and presenting it
  • One-time $60 fee towards the national organization
