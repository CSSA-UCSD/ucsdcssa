How To Get Involved

Interested in Joining the Cognitive Science Student Association?

There are a variety of ways to get involved with CSSA throughout the year. Read below for membership opportunites, and come back Spring Quarter for 2020-2021 Executive Board Member Applications!

General Membership

No commitment is required. You will receive our monthly newsletter and other emails about relevant events. Hope to see you at our events!

Active Volunteers

You must do at least 2 jobs per quarter, including...

  • Tabling
  • Putting up posters
  • Taping flyers to chairs
  • Making class announcements
  • Chalking boards and sidewalks

Committee Members

You report to your Committee lead who is a member of the Executive Board. You will shadow and assist leads with completing tasks. Committees can include...

  • Fundraising
  • Newsletter
  • Sponsorship
