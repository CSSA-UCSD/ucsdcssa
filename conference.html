National Cognitive Science Conference 2020

Innovations in Intelligence

April 5th, 2020 | 9AM - 5PM | Price Center

UC San Diego's Cognitive Science Student Association invites you to join our 15th annual Cognitive Science Conference! Attendees and presenters include distinguished researchers and scientists, reputable companies in related industries, and lab representatives.
